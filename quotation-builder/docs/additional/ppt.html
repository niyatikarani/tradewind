AI Config Suggestion
Based on similar Kosher spice orders to UAE, exporters use: 30 units per inner box · 8 inner boxes per master carton · EUR pallets · 4-layer stack.
  • Photo → dimensions
    Photograph your product next to a coin. AI estimates L×W×H and pre-fills the configurator. No spec sheet needed.
  • Orientation rules
    "This side up", liquids, and fragile items are blocked from rotation. The algorithm respects this — no wrong-side-up cartons reaching the floor.
  • 20ft vs 40ft guidance
    When utilisation drops below your threshold, the system calculates whether adding volume or switching container size saves money — before you commit the booking.
